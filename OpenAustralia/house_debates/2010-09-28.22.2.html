Tuesday, 28 September 2010

Shadow Ministerial Arrangements

4:04 pm

Tony Abbott (Warringah, Liberal Party, Leader of the Opposition) | Hansard source

Mr Speaker, I have the honour to inform the House that the parliamentary Liberal Party has elected me as its leader and the honourable member for Curtin as deputy leader. The honourable member for Leichhardt has been appointed Chief Opposition Whip and the honourable members for Forrest and Barker have been appointed as opposition whips. I understand that a full list of the shadow ministry will be published in the Hansard .
