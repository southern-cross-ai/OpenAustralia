House debates

Tuesday, 22 November 2011

Personal Explanations

3:35 pm

Bob Baldwin (Paterson, Liberal Party, Shadow Minister for Tourism) | Hansard source

Mr Speaker, I wish to make a personal explanation.

Harry Jenkins (Speaker) | Hansard source

Does the honourable member claim to have been misrepresented?

Bob Baldwin (Paterson, Liberal Party, Shadow Minister for Tourism) | Hansard source

Absolutely.

Harry Jenkins (Speaker) | Hansard source

Please proceed.

Bob Baldwin (Paterson, Liberal Party, Shadow Minister for Tourism) | Hansard source

During question time today, the Minister for Health and Ageing said that I had supported the Raymond Terrace GP superclinic. The fact is that, during the election, when the announcement was made I opposed it in the media. The fact is that, since the election, I have opposed it. And the fact is that this government is not building a GP superclinic in Raymond Terrace; it is providing funding for the New South Wales government—

Harry Jenkins (Speaker) | Hansard source

The member will resume his seat.

Natasha Griggs (Solomon, Country Liberal Party) | Hansard source

Mr Speaker, I would like to make a personal explanation.

Harry Jenkins (Speaker) | Hansard source

Does the honourable member claim to have been misrepresented?

Natasha Griggs (Solomon, Country Liberal Party) | Hansard source

Yes.

Harry Jenkins (Speaker) | Hansard source

Please proceed.

Natasha Griggs (Solomon, Country Liberal Party) | Hansard source

In today's Northern Territory News , on page 4, it claims that I have not made up my mind to stand at the next election. This is incorrect. I am absolutely committed to my electorate and I will be standing for preselection when it is called for.

Harry Jenkins (Speaker) | Hansard source

I thank the member for Solomon.

3:36 pm
