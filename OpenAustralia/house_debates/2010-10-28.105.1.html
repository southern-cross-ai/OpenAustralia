Personal Explanations

4:34 pm

Bruce Billson (Dunkley, Liberal Party, Shadow Minister for Small Business, Competition Policy and Consumer Affairs) | Hansard source

Mr Speaker, I wish to make a personal explanation.

Harry Jenkins (Speaker) | Hansard source

Does the honourable member claim to have been misrepresented?

Bruce Billson (Dunkley, Liberal Party, Shadow Minister for Small Business, Competition Policy and Consumer Affairs) | Hansard source

Yes.

Harry Jenkins (Speaker) | Hansard source

Please proceed.

Bruce Billson (Dunkley, Liberal Party, Shadow Minister for Small Business, Competition Policy and Consumer Affairs) | Hansard source

Harry Jenkins (Speaker) | Hansard source

Bruce Billson (Dunkley, Liberal Party, Shadow Minister for Small Business, Competition Policy and Consumer Affairs) | Hansard source

Mr Billson interjecting

Craig Emerson (Rankin, Australian Labor Party, Minister for Trade) | Hansard source

Dr Emerson interjecting

Harry Jenkins (Speaker) | Hansard source

Bruce Billson (Dunkley, Liberal Party, Shadow Minister for Small Business, Competition Policy and Consumer Affairs) | Hansard source

Harry Jenkins (Speaker) | Hansard source

Order! The member will resume his seat.
