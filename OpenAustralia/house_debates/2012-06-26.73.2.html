Questions to the Speaker

Media

3:13 pm

Anthony Albanese (Grayndler, Australian Labor Party, Leader of the House) | Hansard source

Deputy Speaker, for your consideration of whether it is appropriate to incorporate the tabling of the website in Hansard , for your information the Federal Court have put all the documents up on a special page on their website today, available for all to read.

Mrs Bronwyn Bishop (Mackellar, Liberal Party, Shadow Minister for Seniors) | Hansard source

Madam Deputy Speaker, I raise a point of order. The point just raised by the Leader of the House does not address the question of the sub judice rule as it is set out in Practice . Practice simply says when it is acceptable or not acceptable to mention matters before the court in this place. It does not talk about whether or not things are up on websites or anything else. It is quite clear on page 506 of Practice that once it is before a court it may not be dealt with in this place.

Ms Anna Burke (Chisholm, Deputy-Speaker) | Hansard source

I thank the member for Mackellar for her assistance. As I say, I will be seeking advice on whether it should be incorporated or not and I will take on board the information provided by both sides of the chamber.
