Questions without Notice

Liberal Party: Leadership

2:05 pm

Kevin Rudd (Griffith, Australian Labor Party, Leader of the Opposition) | Hansard source

My question again is to the Prime Minister. Will the Prime Minister be up-front with the Australian people and tell them whether he intends to serve a full three-year term if he is chosen to lead this country at the upcoming election?

John Howard (Bennelong, Liberal Party, Prime Minister) | Hansard source
